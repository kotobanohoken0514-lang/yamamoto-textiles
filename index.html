— 私たちについて / ABOUT— ABOUT YAMAMOTO TEXTILES

本物Authentic
の素材だけが持つ力 materials carry true power

福岡を拠点とする山本繊維は、創業60年の糸卸元です。大島紬用の撚糸に特化し、奄美大島や鹿児島の織元・染色職人とともに歩みながら、その伝統の裏側を支えてきました。

Yamamoto Textiles is a yarn-twisting factory specializing in Oshima Tsumugi, working alongside weavers and dye craftspeople across Amami Oshima and Kagoshima. Based in Fukuoka, with yarn-twisting as our core, we are now beginning a new challenge — bringing the materials born through collaboration with Amami's artisans into new forms.

The fabric born through years of collaboration with Amami Oshima's dye craftspeople carries a distinctive black and depth of color that mass production cannot replicate. Where that color comes from — it lives within techniques and relationships built over a long time.

— 素材の背景 / MATERIAL STORY— THE MATERIAL STORY

糸から始まる、
すべての価値

Every value
begins with
the thread

撚糸という工程を通じて、糸は大島紬としての表情を持ち始める。奄美の自然と職人の技が生む色彩を、私たちは新たな形へと昇華させます。

Yamamoto Textiles' strength lies in its yarn-twisting expertise specialized for Oshima Tsumugi, and deep partnerships with weavers and dye craftspeople across Amami Oshima and Kagoshima. Through yarn-twisting, mere thread transforms into the material for Oshima Tsumugi — a living art form.

01

撚糸 — YARN TWISTING

山本繊維独自の撚糸技術。生糸に適度なテンションをかけることで、大島紬に特化した絹糸を生み出します。

Yamamoto's proprietary yarn-twisting technique. By applying precise tension to raw silk, we create the distinctive luster and strength of Oshima Tsumugi.

02

黒 — THE BLACK

奄美の泥田と車輪梅を使い、何十回も染めと泥漬けを繰り返すことで生まれるこの黒は、世界でもここにしか存在しない色です。

A distinctive black unique to Yamamoto Textiles' fabric. Born through years of craft and trade, this color carries a depth and stillness that ready-made products cannot replicate — the crystallization of accumulated technique.

03

新たな価値へ — NEW VALUE

NEW VALUE

伝統素材を礼服・香り袋・アクセサリーへと昇華。着物のルールを超えた、現代人の暮らしに寄り添う新しい形。

Elevating traditional materials into ceremonial dress, fragrance sachets, and accessories. New forms that integrate into modern life beyond kimono conventions.
